## FREE-TO-PAID CONVERSION FUNNEL

### Current Conversion Rate
- Free-to-paid conversion rate: 8.4% (trailing 90 days)
- Previous rate: 11.2% (trailing 90 days as of March 2026)
- Absolute decline: -2.8 percentage points
- Relative decline: -25.0%

### Conversion Funnel Stages (June 2026 cohort, first 30 days)
- Free signups: 4,847
- Completed onboarding (created first project): 2,913 (60.1% — down from 71.4% in March)
- Invited at least 1 team member: 1,641 (33.9% of signups)
- Used product 3+ days in first week (activation threshold): 1,287 (26.6% — down from 34.2% in March)
- Reached paywall (hit feature limit): 894 (18.5%)
- Converted to paid (within 30 days): 407 (8.4%)

### Conversion Drop-Off Analysis
- Biggest drop-off point: Between "completed onboarding" and "activated" (users who don't return after day 1)
- Onboarding completion rate dropped 11.3 pts since March — primary cause: new user confusion (corroborated by support ticket data)
- Users who invite a team member convert at 19.2% vs. 4.1% for solo users
- Time to first conversion: median 8 days (up from 6 days in March — users taking longer to see value)

### Conversion by Acquisition Channel (June cohort)
- Organic search: 11.3% conversion rate
- Direct/referral: 10.8% conversion rate
- Paid social: 6.9% conversion rate
- Product Hunt / review sites: 14.2% conversion rate
- Email campaigns: 9.1% conversion rate

### Failed Conversion Reasons (Exit Survey — 312 responses)
1. "Too complicated to set up" — 34%
2. "Couldn't get my team to adopt it" — 22%
3. "Missing integration I needed" — 19%
4. "Mobile app didn't work well enough" — 14%
5. "Found a cheaper alternative" — 11%
